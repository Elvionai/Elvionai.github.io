Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 2025   |   For Elvion AI and all branches (including Philadelphia AI)

Your Privacy, Your Trust: Always First

We believe privacy rights are fundamental. This page explains how Elvion AI and our associated branches handle your data with care, transparency, and top-tier security.

1. What Data We Collect

  • Account Info: Name, email, country, and profile photo if you register—only when you provide it
  • Service Usage: Messages, AI interactions, usage statistics—for service quality and improvement
  • Technical Data: Device/browser details, IP, logs—for performance, troubleshooting, and security
  • Cookies: Session, analytics, and settings to personalize and secure your experience

2. How We Use Data

  • Deliver and improve the Elvion AI experience—including personalized results and new features
  • Authenticate, safeguard your account, and prevent misuse
  • Analyze trends and stats to build a smarter platform
  • Communicate updates and respond to questions
  • Comply with law, ethics, and safety requirements

3. Sharing & Disclosure

  • No selling of your personal data, ever.
  • Trusted partners (cloud, analytics) see the minimum required; all are under strict confidentiality
  • Legal/safety disclosures only when absolutely required

4. Your Controls & Rights

  • Edit your account/profile from your dashboard
  • Request deletion, export, or review of your data at any time
  • Manage cookie/notification preferences in your settings/browser

5. Security

  • End-to-end encryption + best practice security at the infrastructure level
  • We cannot guarantee perfect safety online—protect your password and use secure devices

6. Responsible AI Use

  • Chats and prompts may be securely stored for quality, improvement, and safety checks
  • No medical, legal, or financial advice—our AI is not a substitute for licensed services

7. For Parents & Children

Elvion AI is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has signed up, contact us—removal is fast and friendly.

8. Policy Changes

We may update this page as Elvion evolves. If changes are significant, we’ll notify you here and/or by email.

9. Contact

Questions or requests? Reach us at privacy@elvionai.com and we’ll respond with care.

Thank you for trusting Elvion AI.
Where intelligence is powerful—and privacy is personal.
