    The "DM for Price" Epidemic: How to Automate Your Sales and Stop Losing Customers in 2026

    Elvion Team Jun 21, 2026 Calculating read time...

    🔊 Listen to this Article

    You know the exact drill. You spend two hours taking the perfect picture of your product. You edit it, write a nice caption, add thirty hashtags, and post it on Instagram or TikTok.

    Then the comments start rolling in.

    "How much?"
    "Price please."
    "Please DM price."

    You get excited. You think you are about to make a lot of money today. So you spend the next hour sending direct messages to forty different people. You tell them the price. And then, absolute silence. Thirty people ignore you completely. Five people tell you they will get back to you next week. The remaining five try to price a 20k item down to 5k.

    You end the day exhausted, staring at your screen, with zero actual sales in your bank account.

    Welcome to the reality of running a "DM for price" business. For years, African vendors, creators, and freelancers have run their entire operations through direct messages and WhatsApp statuses. It was fine when the internet was still young. But in 2026, this informal way of doing business is actively killing your brand, chasing away premium clients, and capping your revenue.

    If your entire business relies on you manually typing out account numbers and prices every single time someone wants to buy something, we need to talk. You are hustling backward. Here is why you need to stop hiding your prices and the exact tools you can use to automate your business this weekend.

    The "Billing by Profile Picture" Problem

    Let us be honest about why vendors hide their prices. Some do it because a random business coach told them it drives engagement. Some do it because they are scared their competitors will steal their pricing strategy. But a lot of vendors do it so they can adjust the price based on how rich the customer looks in their profile picture.

    Customers are not stupid. They know exactly why you are doing it, and they absolutely hate it.

    When you force a potential buyer to jump through hoops just to find out how much your service costs, you create unnecessary friction. Premium clients hate friction. People who are ready to spend good money value their time more than anything else. They do not want to exchange pleasantries and wait six hours for a reply just to find out if your service fits their budget.

    When a high paying client sees "DM for price," they assume you are going to overcharge them. It immediately breaks trust before the transaction even begins.

    A serious business displays its value proudly. When you have a dedicated link where all your services, prices, and terms are clearly listed, you filter out the unserious people. The clients who do contact you will already know your worth and be ready to pay.

    Why You Need a Digital Storefront or Landing Page

    You are a human being, you get tired. You need to sleep. You enter areas with bad network coverage. You have days where you just do not want to talk to anybody.

    If your business requires you to be awake and holding your phone to make a sale, you do not have a business. You have a high stress job where you are your own wicked boss.

    A digital storefront or a landing page is your digital employee. It does not sleep. It does not get tired. At 3 AM, while you are snoring, a customer can click the link in your bio, read the description of your product, see the price, input their card details, and pay. You wake up the next morning to a credit alert and a shipping address. That is how modern commerce works.

    But I know what you are thinking. You are probably saying, "I do not have the money to hire a developer to build a full website for me right now."

    That is completely fine. You do not need a custom domain or a massive budget to automate your sales in 2026. There are tools built exactly for where you are right now. Let us break them down based on what you actually need.

    The Best Free and Cheap Tools for Product Vendors

    If you sell physical products like clothes, food, or skincare, or digital products like ebooks and courses, you need a simple storefront. You just need a place to upload pictures, set prices, and collect money. You do not need to spend a dime to set this up.

    • Paystack Storefront or Flutterwave Store: These are absolute lifesavers for Nigerian and African businesses. Both platforms allow you to create a free digital storefront. You simply create an account, upload your product images, type in the prices, and they generate a beautiful link for you. Put that link in your Instagram bio. When people click it, they see your catalog, add items to their cart, and pay. The payment processor takes a very tiny percentage of the sale, and you get your money in your bank account the next day.
    • Selar: If you sell digital products, templates, or consultations, Selar is incredibly powerful. It is free to start. You upload your ebook or link your consultation calendar, set your price in different currencies, and Selar handles the rest. Your customers in the UK can pay in Pounds, and you will receive Naira. It takes away all the headache of currency conversion.

    Landing Pages for Service Providers and Freelancers

    If you are a freelancer, a graphic designer, a makeup artist, or a writer, a simple product store might not work for you. You need a landing page. You need a place to show off your previous work, explain your packages, and let people book you.

    • Carrd: Carrd is a fantastic tool for building simple, one page websites. It is extremely cheap, and they even have a free tier. You can use their templates to drag and drop a beautiful landing page together in a few hours. You can put a short biography, link your best past projects, state your starting prices, and add a contact form.
    • Linktree or Taplink: If building a page feels too complicated, at the bare minimum, use a link aggregator. Do not just put your WhatsApp number in your bio. Use a tool like Linktree to create a mini menu. You can have buttons that say "View My Portfolio," "See My Pricing Guide," and "Chat on WhatsApp." It instantly makes you look more organized.
    • Elvion Website Studio: If you do not have the money for a full fledged website yet but you want a clean landing page instantly, this is a great free option. It is an AI tool on the Elvion platform. You do not need to code or use templates. You just type what you need. Tell it you want a minimalist portfolio with a pricing section and a contact form, and it generates the code for a functional landing page right there. It does not do custom domains or heavy corporate hosting, but for a fast, free digital footprint, it works perfectly.

    Setting Up Your Automated Funnel

    Having the link is only step one. Now you have to train your customers to use it. You have to stop enabling bad behavior.

    When you post a new product and someone comments "Price," do not send them a direct message. Reply publicly and say:

    "Hi! This item is 15,000 Naira. You can order it directly through the link in our bio!"

    Next, automate your direct messages. Go to your Instagram and WhatsApp Business settings and set up an auto reply. When someone sends you a message that says "Hello," your account should instantly reply with something like:

    "Welcome to our store! We are currently attending to orders. To see all our available items, check our prices, and place an order immediately, please click the link in our bio. If you have a specific complaint, please leave a message and a human agent will reply shortly."

    This changes everything. The serious buyers will click the link and buy. The time wasters will read the automated message and leave you alone. You will reclaim hours of your life every single day.

    You are a serious business owner. Your time is valuable. Your products and services are top notch. It is time your business operations reflected that reality. Stop relying on manual labor, stop hiding your prices, and start building automated systems.

    Pick one of the tools mentioned above, set it up this weekend, and watch how your business transforms.
