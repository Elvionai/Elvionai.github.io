Privacy Policy

Your privacy and trust are essential. This policy explains how Elvion AI (and all its branches, including Philadelphia AI) collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: When you register or log in, we collect your name, email, and (optionally) profile photo. This is used for authentication and personalization.
  • Usage Data: Interactions with Elvion services (messages, prompts, results, usage stats) to improve our platforms and user experience.
  • Device & Log Data: Browser, device type, IP, and technical logs for security and troubleshooting.
  • Cookies: Used for session memory, preferences, and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, personalize, and improve the Elvion ecosystem experience.
  • To secure your account and prevent unauthorized access.
  • To analyze trends, usage, and power new features.
  • To send important updates or communicate with you.
  • To comply with policy, law, and ethical standards.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • Some data is shared with trusted service providers (hosting, analytics) with strict non-disclosure agreements.
  • Disclosures may occur for legal, safety, or security requirements.

4. Your Choices and Rights

  • You can update account info anytime from your profile.
  • You can delete your account, request correction, or request a copy of your information.
  • You can manage cookie preferences in your browser.
  • Control notification/communication preferences via your settings.

5. Data Security

  • Modern, industry-grade security (encryption, access control, cloud compliance).
  • No system is 100% secure. Protect your credentials and avoid sharing sensitive info in chat.

6. AI and Content Use

  • Interactions with AI models may be stored and reviewed for quality, accuracy, and safety improvements.
  • Elvion and branches (like Philadelphia AI) do not use data for third-party training unless you've consented.
  • Never rely on the AI for medical, legal, investment, or critical advice.

7. Children’s Privacy

Elvion AI platforms are not intended for users under 13. If we become aware of a child’s information, we will promptly remove it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Significant changes will be communicated on the site or by email. Continuing to use Elvion after updates means you accept the new policy.

9. Contact Us

For privacy questions or requests: privacy@elvionai.com

Elvion is committed to transparency, trust, and empowering your digital future.
