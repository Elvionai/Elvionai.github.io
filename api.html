Authentication: The Bearer's Sigil

To traverse the protected realms of Elvion AI, your every request must be signed with a valid API Key. This key is transmitted as a Bearer Token within the `Authorization` header. Think of it as your digital sigil, a mark of authenticity that grants you passage and power within our systems.

Authorization: Bearer YOUR_ELVION_API_KEY

Each invocation of a protected endpoint consumes credits from your account balance. Should your credits be depleted, the API will respond with a `402 Payment Required` status, a cosmic signal that your energy reserves must be replenished to continue your work.

Error Handling: Navigating Anomalies

Even in the most advanced systems, cosmic anomalies can occur. Understanding these signals is key to a smooth development journey. Here are the common status codes and their meanings.

400 Bad Request
The server could not understand your request. This is usually caused by malformed JSON or missing required parameters in the request body (like message or prompt). Check your payload against the documentation.

401 Unauthorized
Your API Key is missing, invalid, or expired. Ensure your `Authorization: Bearer YOUR_ELVION_API_KEY` header is correctly formatted and uses a valid key.

402 Payment Required
Your account has insufficient credits to perform the requested action. Please visit your developer dashboard to recharge.

500 Internal Server Error
An unexpected error occurred on our end. This is a problem with our servers, not your request. These are typically rare and fleeting. If the problem persists, please contact support with the details of your request.
